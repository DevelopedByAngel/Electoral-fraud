What is Eradication of electoral fraud?

The main objective is to prevent malpractices during polling of votes using the concept of biometric identification. Due to polling of fake votes the nation may be handed to in the hands of wrong people which can be prevented by reducing the polling of fake votes.Every human has a unique iris and fingerprint and these details are already stored in the aadhaar database of the Indian government.These details can be cross checked and then the voter is allowed to do his/her civic duty of voting.

The Process

Beside the polling machine a machine containing touch sensitive panel and biometric scanner is placed. The voter is first instructed to place his/her thumb in a biometric scanner to scan his unique fingerprint details (loop, whorl or arch) ‌Now the data will be passed to the aadhaar database where the person’s fingerprint is verified .For second step verification the iris size is kept as a backup database and the person’s information is cross checked with the iris database.The information that this citizen has casted is vote is stored in block chain cloud servers. After this process of confirmation of an individual, the voter is permitted to execute his/her civic duty of voting. Polling has two packages .One of them stores the data about vote added to some specific party/individual candidate .Other holds the data that a individual has voted and entry of another individual with the same data is restricted.
